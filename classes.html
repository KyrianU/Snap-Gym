Workout Classes

Strengh workout

A full-body workout design to help improve body strength. Strength workout uses a variety of resistance equipment like dumbells, kettlebells, resistance bands and body weight to help all muscle group to grow and become stronger

Circuit workout

Circuit workouts are high intensity, high energy training design to help with body toning and lose body fat. It's a combination of 5-6 exercises, performed with 30 seconds rest periods.



HIIT Workout

HIIT, which stands for High intensity Internval Training, is a short bust high intensity training, usually lasting between 45-60 seconds, followed by a resting period of 2-3 mins



Cycle

Cycle, commonly known as spin workout, is a cycling workout which focuses on strength, endurance, high intensity, intervals and recovery. Excerices ranges from hill climb, time trials, long distance and sprints
